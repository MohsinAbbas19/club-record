Frequently Asked Questions

How does the flexible payment system work?

Our system allows customers to pay any amount they want towards a product, without any fixed percentage requirements. Customers can make payments of $50, $100, $500, or any amount they choose, at any time, until the product is fully paid off.

What platforms does the plugin support?

Our plugin is compatible with major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and custom PHP/JavaScript solutions. We also provide API integration for custom platforms.

How secure are the payment transactions?

We use bank-grade security with end-to-end encryption, PCI DSS compliance, and advanced fraud detection. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways with real-time monitoring.

Can I customize the payment interface?

Yes! The plugin is fully customizable. You can modify colors, text, layout, and branding to match your website's design. We also offer custom development services for advanced customizations.

What happens if a customer doesn't complete payment?

The plugin includes automated reminder systems, payment tracking, and flexible policies you can set. You can configure grace periods, late fees, and automatic notifications to encourage completion.
