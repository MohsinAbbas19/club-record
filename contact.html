Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the integration process take?

The integration process typically takes 2-4 hours for standard e-commerce platforms like Shopify or WooCommerce. For custom implementations, it may take 1-2 business days depending on your specific requirements.

What payment gateways are supported?

We support all major payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Square, Authorize.net, and many regional payment providers. Custom gateway integration is also available.

Is there a setup fee or monthly charge?

Our plugin offers flexible pricing models. You can choose from one-time license fees or subscription-based pricing depending on your business needs. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

Can customers set their own installment amounts?

Yes! That's the core feature of our plugin. Customers can pay any amount they want as an installment, without any fixed percentage restrictions. They have complete flexibility in managing their payments.

Do you provide technical support after implementation?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive technical support including 24/7 live chat, email support, phone support, and detailed documentation. Our team is always ready to help you.
